Solar-Powered Propulsion

Solar Burst utilizes advanced solar sail technology to capture and harness solar radiation, enabling continuous acceleration and propulsion without the need for traditional rocket fuel.

High Payload Capacity

Despite its eco-friendly design, Solar Burst boasts a substantial payload capacity, making it ideal for deploying satellites, conducting scientific missions, and even supporting future crewed missions to distant celestial bodies.

Modular Design

The rocket's modular architecture allows for flexibility in mission planning and payload configuration, accommodating diverse scientific instruments, communication equipment, and satellite payloads.
